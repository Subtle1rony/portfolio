Technical Artist

At Sledgehammer Games I worked with the environment team on Call of Duty: WWII. My primary contributions were to environment art, which included modeling, texturing and composition from a designer blockout. Lighting and vfx were handled by their respective teams.

Market Garden

For multiplayer map Market Garden I modeled and textured the exterior of the mansion as well as the interior of the Glass Room and placed the fallen parachutes around the map using cloth simulation. The parachute cords were also made with a procedural Houdini tool.

Battleship

Battleship was the first map I worked on at Sledgehammer. I was given a few structures near the stern of the ship to build as I learned to use their proprietary game engine.
